## Performance Different Annotators
To ensure the reliability of the dataset and filter out potential malicious annotations, we calculated the mean, variance, and standard deviation of the scores assigned by five individual human annotators. This statistical oversight ensures that each annotator's output remains within a reasonable and consistent range. You can see the result in Table \ref{tab:annotators}.
